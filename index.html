Juicebox

Bio:

Juicebox was found roaming the streets of Texas as a baby, and was spirited away to the Seattle Humane Society, where he first met Kayla. Kayla was trying to get over a difficult breakup, and thought a dog might soothe her troubled soul. Although she had intended to get a medium-sized, or even small, dog, Juicebox stole her heart with his adorable puppy ways, and the two became fast friends. He's pretty much the best dog. He is what is commonly called a "mutt" or "mongrel", or simply "not inbred", with ancestors ranging from the Karelian Bear Dogs of Finland, to the Rottweilers of ancient Rome, to the Huskies of Siberia. Whatever his ancestry, he is just about the sweetest and best dog you could possibly ask for.

Name Origin:

When Juicebox was first brought home, he was not yet house trained. One day, while peeing on the floor in the common room of my co-op, one of my housemates commented "Wow, it just keeps coming! He's like a little juicebox!" The rest, as they say, is history.
